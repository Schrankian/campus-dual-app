This app tries to wrap the Campus-Dual Website into a nice looking ui on android. 
All data is synced locally an only known to you and the University-Server. 
Currently supported features:
- Overview of general information like Semester, Credits, etc...
- Overview of completed exams and grade distributions
- Integrated timetable (in the app or as widget)
- Shows some important key points, like upcoming exams or new test results
- Available offline (but needs to be online to sync the data)
